Our Journey

From a Simple Mission to a Trusted Partner

Founded in 2022, Phoenix Leads Solutions was built on a clear purpose: to connect consumers with trusted insurance solutions by delivering high-quality, exclusive leads, inbound calls, and live transfers for Auto Insurance, Medicare, and Final Expense. Through compliant marketing and data-driven strategies, we help our partners grow while providing consumers with a seamless experience.

Our vision is to become the most trusted and reliable lead generation partner in the insurance industry — recognized for transparency, compliance, and consistently delivering exclusive, high-intent opportunities that drive long‑term success for our clients. We measure our success not by the volume of leads we send, but by the lasting relationships we help our partners build.

Core Values

What Guides Everything We Do

These aren't posters on a wall. They're the operating principles we hire, fire, and reward by.

01

Radical Transparency

We share data, dashboards, and decision-making openly with clients. No smoke and mirrors — just honest communication about what's working and what isn't.

02

Ownership Mentality

We treat your pipeline like our own. Every team member is empowered to make decisions that drive client outcomes, not just follow a playbook.

03

Continuous Evolution

We test, learn, and iterate relentlessly. What worked last quarter may not work next quarter, so we stay ahead of channel shifts and buyer behavior.

04

Quality Over Quantity

We'd rather deliver 10 highly-qualified meetings than 100 unqualified contacts. Our targeting and vetting processes are obsessive — and it shows in conversion rates.

05

Client as Partner

We don't do one-off transactions. We build long-term relationships with clients who treat us as an extension of their team — and we do the same in return.

06

Security & Compliance

Data protection isn't optional. We operate under strict GDPR, CAN-SPAM, and TCPA guidelines, with full audit trails and enterprise-grade security protocols.
